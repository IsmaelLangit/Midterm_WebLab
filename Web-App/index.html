LibWaze

LibWaze is a web-application that helps students in Saint Louis University, Baguio City - Main Campus in navigating on the three floors of the library in Msgr. Charles Vath Building. Students can search books by book ID or find a specific sub-section of the library, then LibWaze will show you the route or way going to it.

EASY

The interface is easy to understand and use.

INTERACTIVE

When you start finding the location of the book or subsection, with one click, it shows the route on the map.
